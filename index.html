Internship Overview

Our web developer internship program at Cognifyz Technologies provides hands-on experience in web development. Interns will work on real projects, collaborate with experienced developers, and gain valuable skills in a supportive environment. The internship duration is 3 months, starting from June 1, 2023.

Internship Highlights

Joining our internship program comes with exciting benefits:

  • Real-World Experience

    Work on actual projects, contributing to our development efforts and gaining practical skills.

  • Mentorship

    Receive guidance and mentorship from experienced developers, fostering your professional growth.

  • Team Collaboration

    Collaborate with our skilled development team, enhancing your teamwork and communication skills.

Qualifications

We are looking for candidates with the following qualifications and skills:

  • Strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Vue)
  • Basic understanding of back-end development and databases
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to work well in a collaborative team environment
